Raid Rules

All Raid Announcements will be made in the #raid_announcements channel on the Sigma Discord server, you MUST check these notifications before each raid and make sure to follow the instructions posted by our officers. Not reading these notifications is NOT an excuse for breaking the rules.

Raid Info

T7 Rancor Raid:
  • Raid will launch at 8:00 PM ET
  • For the first 24 hours, everyone must post ZERO DAMAGE (guarantees everyone will get rewards). Any player that posts damage other than ZERO before this 24 hour period will be removed/re-invited to the guild as penalty.
  • At the end of 24 hours(at guild reset), we will confirm that no one has posted damage and at 8:00 PM ET an officer will announce “Raid is now OPEN“!!
  • At that point you may begin attacking the raid but there is Absolutely NO damage posting allowed for the first 20 minutes of the Raid. You MUST wait until 8:20 PM ET to post ANY damage
  • If you are able to enter P4 or are capable of soloing the entire raid you MAY NOT post damage until 8:30PM.

Discipline

In Mandalor we follow the 3 strike policy.

This means that with the start of each month, any strikes that you may have had are wiped clean. If you get two strikes in 1 month, those will not carry over to the next month.

  • 1st: Kick/re-invite
  • 2nd: Kick/re-invite
  • 3rd: Kick/re-invite, consideration for guild removal by the leadership.
In rare circumstances where a rule break may be extremely minute, an officer may issue you a penalty less severe in the likes of a 0 post or non participation on the next raid. But be aware that a kick/re-invite is our official policy, so stick to the rules!

Be respectful of your fellow guild mates and remember to treat everyone with courtesy, respect, and in a manner you yourself would wish to be treated. Toxic comments or blatant disrespect directed at any of your guild mates will NOT be tolerated.

Our rules, be they pertaining to raids or general guild conduct and contribution, are designed to create a level playing field for all of our players.

600 Contribution

Getting your 600 Daily is a mandatory part of being a member in our guild. Please make sure you are willing to meet that level of commitment before joining our guild.

If you know ahead of time that you will miss your 600 for whatever reason (ex: traveling in a no reception area), it is your responsibility to post ahead of time in #leave_of_absence (@mention your officers) and arrange for someone else to cover your 600 contribution. If you notify the officer staff in advance, we can bring in a Ticket Merc while you are gone and generate those tickets you would have otherwise missed.

Missing your 600 contribution will be addressed with the same 3 strike policy:

  • 1st: Kick/re-invite
  • 2nd: Kick/re-invite
  • 3rd: Kick/re-invite, consideration for guild removal by the leadership.
