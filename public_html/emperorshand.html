About Us

Emperor's Hand was the fourth guild in the SIGMA ALLIANCE.
  • PrigKeeNoo leads Emperor's Hand, and he has many officers in support roles:
  • Raid Masters: Tyron Brandt and Aerohead
  • Discipline: Muggin
  • Recruiting: Blackfish
  • TB/TW Masters: PrigKeeNoo, Blackfish, Zabernat
  • Guild GP: 100+ Million
  • Emperor's Hand has a guild reset at 5:30pm (UTC -4, Eastern Daylight Time)
  • Light Side TB: 37 stars
  • Dark Side TB: 37 stars

Requirements

GP: 2,000,000+
Characters: 5* Captain Han Solo, 6* Hoth Rebel Scout, 5* Hoth Rebel Soldier, 5* Colonel Starck, 5* General Veers, 5* Snowtrooper (Must be able to complete Imperial Probe Droid Mission)
600 tickets per day
Active on Discord

Discord and Conduct

  • Check Discord Daily
  • Keep content PG as we have young players among us (except in specific non pg areas)
  • Report Bugs or Errors in the server
  • Don’t Troll
  • Be Respectful and Polite towards others

Tickets

  • 600 Tickets required daily
  • If you miss you get a strike (see below)
  • Post in #leave_of_absence if you’ll be away from the game and unable to complete tickets

24 Galactic War Battles Only

On every Tuesday-Wednesday the guild activity is GW battles. It is possible to achieve 36 battles if you save Mondays battle for Tuesday however you are forbidden to do more than 24 battles. This gives everyone the option to reach rank 1 on the activity. You are not required to do 24 but must not go over.

Territory Battles

Members are sorted into SIGMA Alliance Guilds based on Territory Battle performance. You must always do your very best.

Territory Wars

Sign up is not mandatory, but if you do sign up, participation IS mandatory.

Miscellaneous

  • If you know you will be away from the game in a way that adversely affects your performance in TB, TW, tickets, or other activities in game, please post a message in the #leave-of-absence channel on the Sigma discord server.
  • As part of Sigma, there is plenty of movement up. There are also officer opportunities for those interested.

Strikes

  • 1st Miss (Less than 10 tickets): Warning
  • 1st Miss (more than 10 points): Zero damage on the next Rancor
  • 2nd Miss: Zero damage on the next hAAT
  • 3rd Miss: Sitting out Rancor completely
  • 4th Miss: Sitting out hAAT and Rancor completely
  • 5th Miss: TBD based on overall performance (this can include up to guild transfer to a non 600 strict guild)
*strikes are tracked on a floating 30 day period.
**Posting damage in raids during a 0 Damage period will result in skipping that raid completely next time ***Raid penalties are equivalent to miss's ****officers reserve the right to compound strike penalties.
