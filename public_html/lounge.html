About Us

The Lounge is an extremely casual guild for you to play in if you aren’t active enough for a hardcore guild. We run T6/T7 rancor raids and nAAT. If you’re intersted in joining, join our server and PM Chipwexler or Sagenod.

Raids are usually launched at 3:30 PM ET with a 0 DMG for 24 hours on Rancor, and ZERG from start on nAAT.
