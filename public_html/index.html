About Us

SIGMA ALLIANCE joined THE ALLIΔNCE in March 2017 as founding member. We are a cluster of guilds, ranging from beginner to extremely experienced. Our aim is to provide a range of guilds under one roof for players all over the world, casual and hardcore, and at any level. We create a fun and interactive atmosphere where you can grow as a player and make friends at the same time. If you have any question about SWGOH, please don’t hesitate, ask SIGMA ALLIANCE.

Our first guild, TeamSkunk Σ, now called SIGMA ALLIANCE α, was created in early August 2016 by SithKnightXzone5. Starting out as a T4 guild, we slowly pushed our way up to being a successful hAAT guild. During our push for hAAT, we needed to replace some of our friends that were building up their rosters slower than others. We didn’t want to lose our friends that didn’t want to go hAAT so soon, so we started an ongoing expansion of guilds to ensure we had a perfect spot for all members. Since then we have created guilds at all levels, in EU, US, and Australian time zones.

SIGMA ALLIANCE prides itself on listening to all its members and having a great community built on friendships and fun. We encourage all SIGMA players to build hSTR rosters, just at their own speed.
