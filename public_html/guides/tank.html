Vaderade's Complete HAAT Guide

Updated 10/2/2017
I've seen so many partial guides or incomplete guides for the HAAT. Here I hope to bring all this information together to answer any and all questions you might have to being the best of the best in the Heroic Tank Raid. If you have questions about the Rancor, please visit this page. This guide is laid out in terms of phases, with each subheader being a new team. Each team guide will have the characters required, alternate or supporting character options, and the mods required to make the raid team work.

Phase 1

This Guy
Probably the easiest team to assemble, this team is composed of just Commander Luke Skywalker. To get Commander Luke Skywalker to work for this phase of the tank, he needs to be at gear level 10 or higher.

Requirements

Currently we are trying to build up our guild roster for the new Territory Battles so new recruits must have at least 30x G10+ Characters to be considered. If you are below this mark but have at least 20x G10+ then Mandolorians or Omega would be a better pick for you. We also require 600 tickets per day. Discord is mandatory; You must check discord at least once per day so that you don’t miss out on announcements affecting you.

Raid Info

T7 Rancor Raid:
  • Raid Launch Is at 5:30pm GMT (Guild Reset)
  • The First 24 hours is 0 Damage
  • At 5:30pm GMT the following day, the raid opens up for ZERG.
  • Once ZERG is called in game chat, you must wait 20 minutes from the time an officer called ZERG. Absolutely NO damage posting is allowed until an officer notifies you that you may post.
  • If you CAN solo the raid, please wait 5 extra minutes.
hAAT Raid:
  • Raid Launch is at 6:30pm GMT (Guild Reset+1)
  • The first 3 phases are open for you to hit as much as you want.
  • Once P4 is reached, the raid pauses and you must wait until P4 ZERG is called at 6:30pm GMT the following day to post
  • P4 ZERG is one team only and you must wait 10 minutes to post your damage
  • If you are unsure of any raid rule, please consult any officer.
Raid Infractions
For small errors (such as posting a small amount of damage in 0 phase) you will simply be asked to post a 0 on the next rancor raid. For larger mistakes (such as posting large amounts of damage before the 20 minute mark) you will be given a strike.

Strike System

This also applies to strikes given for normal rule infractions as well. 1st Strike = Miss Next Rancor Raid 2nd Strike = Miss Next AAT Raid 3rd Strike = Boot After 30 days of having a strike, it will be removed.

Guild Rules

This also applies to strikes given for normal rule infractions as well. 1st Strike = Miss Next Rancor Raid 2nd Strike = Miss Next AAT Raid 3rd Strike = Boot After 30 days of having a strike, it will be removed.
Discord and Conduct
  • Check Discord Daily
  • Keep content PG as we have young players among us (except in specific non pg areas)
  • Report Bugs or Errors in the server
  • Don’t Troll
  • Be Respectful and Polite Towards others
Tickets
  • 600 Tickets required daily
  • If you miss you get a strike
  • Post in #leave_of_absence if you’ll be away from the game and unable to complete tickets
24 Galactic War Battles Only
On every Tuesday-Wednesday the guild activity is GW battles. It is possible to achieve 36 battles if you save Mondays battle for Tuesday however you are forbidden to do more than 24 battles. This gives everyone the option to reach rank 1 on the activity. You are not required to do 24 but must not go over.
Mercing
If you wish to offer yourself as a Raid Merc to another guild, you are allowed to do so but must not miss your tickets for the day. If you will be away from the guild for more than 1 day, please seek permission from an officer so that they can coordinate a Ticket Merc to cover you.
Other Rules
  • Treat your officers with respect. Sometimes a call against you may be unjust or unfair if this is the case it is likely a mistake. Notify the officer politely.
  • Don’t openly attack or accuse another player in-game or on discord. Notify an officer and have them deal with the situation.

You're going to need to choose a strong leader to get you through your first rancor solo. There's 5 good leaders in the current meta (as of 9/20/2017). Those 5 are:

  • Wedge Antilles

  • Darth Vader

  • Teebo

  • Jyn Erso

  • Grand Admiral Thrawn

Pick a Tenacity Down Character

We're going to need a tenacity down character so that our turn meter removal lands as much as possible. In phase 4, the rancor has a 15% base chance to resist all turn meter removal, even with tenacity down. So what are our options for tenacity down? Here's the list:

  • TIE Fighter Pilot

  • Director Krennic

  • Dengar

  • Commander Luke Skywalker
