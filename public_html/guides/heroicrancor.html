Rancor Raid Beginner's Guide

So you want to solo the rancor? Well this guide is going to show you how. I'd like to thank AhnaldT101 for a lot of this content, he put in the leg work for most of it. A strong rancor solo team has 5 elements to it; a leader who's leader ability benefits or bolsters your the rest of the team, a character that can apply tenacity down, a character that can apply speed down, a turn meter removal character, and a bonus support character. I'll list off who fits the best in the following sections. So let's jump right into it!

Choosing a Leader

You're going to need to choose a strong leader to get you through your first rancor solo. There's 5 good leaders in the current meta (as of 9/20/2017). Those 5 are:

  • Wedge Antilles

  • Darth Vader

  • Teebo

  • Jyn Erso

  • Grand Admiral Thrawn

Pick a Tenacity Down Character

We're going to need a tenacity down character so that our turn meter removal lands as much as possible. In phase 4, the rancor has a 15% base chance to resist all turn meter removal, even with tenacity down. So what are our options for tenacity down? Here's the list:

  • TIE Fighter Pilot

  • Director Krennic

  • Dengar

  • Commander Luke Skywalker

Pick a Speed Down Character

Luckily here we have lots of options. Any character that can apply speed down will work for this category. You want speed down on the rancor so that your characters can take as many turns as possible, especially your turn meter removal character. Here's the Speed Down list:

  • Fives

Putting it All TogeTher

After ingesting all this information, it's time to put your knowledge to the test. To create a relatively successful rancor solo team, pick a character from each one of the categories listed above. Be on the lookout for synergy though! A rag tag band of 5 wont do nearly as much damage as a group of 5 with synergy between all of the characters. Zader

  • Darth Vader

  • Jyn Erso

  • TIE Fighter Pilot

  • Grand Moff Tarkin

  • Shoretrooper
The orginal, the classic, the Zader Squad. Based around having Darth Vader's leader ability - Inspiring Through Fear - zeta'd, this squad is the classic turn meter manipulation squad. Requirements for this squad are as follows: Darth Vader must be your leader and must have his Inspiring Through Fear ability at the zeta level so that when Empire and Sith allies attack, they have a 50% chance to remove 20% turn meter. Other heavy turn meter manipulation units on this squad are Grand Moff Tarkin and Jyn Erso. As far as mod requirements go, speed is king. Here's a table of what your minimums should look like for a full solo:

Character Gear Level Speed
Darth Vader G10 150
Jyn Erso G10 200
TIE Fighter Pilot G10 236
Grand Moff Tarkin G10 170
Shoretrooper G11 No speed necessary
